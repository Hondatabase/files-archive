This car does everything on tarseal, grass and gravel including hill climbs, rally sprints, bent sprints, autocross, motokhana, drags and club sport championship.

Got it up to a 175k (110Mph) on a gravel straight in the last rally sprint gets very unstable, going to get the big rear spoiler.

Photos from very muddy auto cross (came second), Tar Seal hill climb (2nd non turbo) 10th overall (40 entries). Marlborough Autocross Series Day 1 - 6th, Day 2 - 3rd, Day 3 - 1st, final result, 2nd overall for the Series.

Last 2 photos taken after spoiler fitted now more stable at 170k, also showing on last 2 photos is damage to left front after hitting a concrete wall.
