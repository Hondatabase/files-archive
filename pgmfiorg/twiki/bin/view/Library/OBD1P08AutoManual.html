RP17 and RP18 values
  Automatic Manual
RP17 2.7K open
RP18 4.7K jumped

this is for board pn: 02d01550-1500

Auto to Manual: remove rp17 remove rp18 add a jumper to rp18

Manual to Auto:

  • remove jumper at RP18 (Not sure what will be here)
  • add 4.7kOhm +/- 5% at RP18 SMD
  • add 2.7kOhm +/- 5% at RP17 SMD
  • add IC15 & IC16 5050 S or equiv.
  • add Caps at C73, c74, c75, c76 (values??? 0.1 uf ? should be ceramic)
  • add Diodes to D13 and D12 (1A Surface mount will work)
  • add q29 q28
  • add c57
- Not sure about any transistors yet...
