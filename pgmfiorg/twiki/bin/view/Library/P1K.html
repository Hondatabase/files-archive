P1J and P1K come from 96-00 UK civics with D14 engines. Despite the years, those are OBDI ECUs and seem to be easyly convertable for vtec

Those OBDI P1J or P1K ECUs share a strange board layout labeled as: "2PU6098-4460P1 5 A8E-A3", but get easyly chipped. Vtec converion seems easy, but is not yet tested.
