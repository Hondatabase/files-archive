The ATMEL AT29C010 chip is a FLASH-ROM chip with 1Mbit(~128kb) of memory. It can be found in old Pentium I&II computer motherbords. This chip will work in ECUs, but require pins to be moved around/altered due to them being a different pinout than either 27 C256 or 29 C256 chips. When writing/burning the program, you need to specify the start address, where your ECU looks at.
