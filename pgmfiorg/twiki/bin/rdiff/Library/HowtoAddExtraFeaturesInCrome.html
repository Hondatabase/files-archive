step 1: click 'down load' on the extra features .zip file found on page 1/post 1 of the 'add extra features' thread.

step 2: after you click 'down load' you will then click 'save' in the next window that pops up

step 3: select the desired location in which you wish to save the .zip file. it doesn't matter where you save it, it only matters where you unzip it to, which you don't have to worry about for a couple more steps.

step 4: go to wherever you saved the .zip file (mine was saved in 'my documents')

step 5: right click on the .zip file and then go down and highlight 'extrac all'

step 6: the extraction wizard will then pop up. click 'next' in the first window that pops up.

step 7: THE IMPORTANT PART!!! now is the important part where you need to actually 'browse' to find the crome plug in directory where the files will be unzipped to. so, click on 'browse'.

step 8: after you click 'browse' you will need to search for the actual crome directory. this isn't where you saved the crome .zip file when you first downloaded it, but where your computer actually unzipped the files to when you installed crome. if you're not sure, you may have to do some looking around, but by default, your computer more than likely put it somewhere under 'program files'. once you find the 'crome' folder, you then need to click on the '+' sign to the left of it in order to view the actual 'plug ins' folder. once you see it, just highlight 'plug ins' and hit 'ok' at the bottom of the window.

step 9: now you will be taken back to the extraction wizard and you will see where the files are about to be unzipped to. you will now click 'next' at the bottom of the window.

step 10: you will now be asked for a password, which is the same password you had to use to install crome.

step 11: once you put in the password, you'll come to last window in the extraction wizard, click 'finish'

step 12: if you have 'show extracted files' checked in the previous step before you click finish, the plug in directory will open up after you click finish. the 'addextrafeatures.cpf' file is what you want to find.

step 13: now open crome and go to your plug in directory and go to 'install new plug in'

step 14: once you click 'install new plug in' you will need to locate your plug in directory. this will be the same exact place that you had to find when you unzipped the 'addextrafeatures.zip' file.

step 15: once you've located the plug in directory, you need to open it and then locate/highlight the 'addextrafeatures.cpf' file, and then click 'open'

step 16: you should now see the 'addextrafeatures.cpf' file in your plug in directory.

step 17: now open the .bin that you want to add extra features to and go to 'plug ins' -> 'enhancements' -> 'add extra features'.

step 18: if you've done everything properly, once you open 'add extra features' in to your .bin, you will see the following window. then click next.

step 19: now go under options and you should see the extra features added at the bottom of the window.
