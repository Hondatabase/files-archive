%META:TOPICINFO{author="blundar" date="1077744191" format="1.0" version="1.1"}% You can find out a lot from reading the label on a OBD1 ECU. This applies mainly to the Civic ECUs... dunno bount the integra ones.

37820-ABC-XYZ

Digits Meaning
ABC Engine ECU designed for
X A: US 49 state, L: US, Cali, G: European
Y 0: 5-speed, 5: Auto
Z even number: "11F0" board, odd number: "1720" board

Example:

37820-P28-A52

  • P28 = SOHC Vtec D16Z6
  • A = US, 49 state
  • 5 = Auto
  • 2 = "11F0" board
