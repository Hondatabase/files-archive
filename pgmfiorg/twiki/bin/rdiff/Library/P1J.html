%META:TOPICINFO{author="1net" date="1170087240" format="1.0" version="1.1"}% %META:TOPICPARENT{name="EcuFamilies"}% P1J and P1K come from 96-00 UK civics with D14 engines. Despite the years, those are OBDI ECUs and seem to be easyly convertable for vtec

Sorry for the bad images, please consult the page on P1 K for better views of the PCB.

Those OBDI P1J or P1K ECUs share a strange board layout labeled as: "2PU6098-4460P1 5 A8E-A3", but get easyly chipped. Vtec converion seems easy, but is not yet tested.
