Describe OBD0 Conversion Formula here.

  • OBD016 Bit RPM is used for revlimiters, target idle and most other 16bit RPM values in the ROM.
  • OBD0 VSS is used to convert the values in ram 06Ch to Vehicle Speed.
  • OBD0 Fuel is used to convert the hex values in the fuel maps to pulse width in milliseconds.
  • OBD0 Ignition is used to convert the hex values in the ignition maps into degrees advance.
  • OBD0 VTEC is used for VTEC values.
  • OBD08 Bit Rev is used for the OBD0 VTEC ECUs' revlimits.
