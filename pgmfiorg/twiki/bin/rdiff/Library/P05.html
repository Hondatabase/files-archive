92-95 OBD1 Civic CX (D15B8)

The P05 ECU came with the D15 SOHC non-vtec motor with close-coupled cat and a 1-wire O2 sensor in the 92-95 Civic CX. Basically an OBD1 version of the 88-91 HF motor. FUEL ECONOMY!!!
