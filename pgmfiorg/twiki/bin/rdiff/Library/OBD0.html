%META:TOPICINFO{author="blundar" date="1077231960" format="1.0" version="1.1"}% "OBD0" is not an official standard like OBD2. It is most usually used to refer to MPFI ECUs made from 88-91 with Electronic Advance Distributors found on OBD0 Civic Integra? cars of this era. Technically, all ECUs made prior to 1988 and all accord/prelude/v6/OBD0 Vtec ECUs of this era are also "OBD0" but due to the drastic differences, they are grouped seperately and referred to as "pre-OBD" ECUs, or another term.

OBD0 ECUs (88-91)

  • PG7 : 88-89 Integra (D16A1)
  • Trash.PM6? : 88-91 Civic/CRX SOHC Si (1.6l sohc)
  • PM7 : 89-91 Civic Si DOHC ZC (JDM EF3)
  • PM8 : 88-91 CRX HF
  • PR3 : 89-91 JDM B16A EF8/9
  • PR5 : 90-91 JDM Integra ZXi (1.6 sohc)
  • PW0 : 89-91 JDM/Euro B16A EF8/9 DA6-XSi (DOHC VTEC)
  • PR4 : 90-91 Integra LS/GS (b18a)
  • PS9 : 88-91 4 door civic EX Auto (sohc 1.6)

These DPFI ECUs were also made during the same time period:

  • PM5 : 88-91 Civic/CRX DX (DPFI D15b)
  • PM9 : 88-91 Civic base model (DPFID15b)
