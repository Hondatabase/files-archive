92-95 "OBD1" Civics and Integras

All 92-95 Civic and Integra ECUs use one of three board designs. Differences in ECUs are often as simple as a different program. Different portions of each "motherboard" can be populated to enable controlling vtec, automatic transmissions or purge solenoids.

  • P07 : 92-95 OBD1 Civic VX (D15Z1) scan (Thanks Katman!) 6 Wire O2 P07 6 Wire O2 P07 6 Wire O2 P07(thanks Deluded / Raver Motorsports)

  • P05 : 92-95 OBD1 Civic CX (D15B)
  • P06 : 92-95 OBD1 Civic DX (D15B) scan (Thanks Katman!)
  • P28 : 92-95 OBD1 Civic Si/Ex (sohc vtec d16z6) scan of auto scan of auto (thanks turboef9) scan of ECU modded by Erick's racing scan of manual ECU USDM/JDM (Thanks Katman)
  • P30 : 92-95 OBD-1 Del Sol? DOHC Vtec Si/EG Si R? ECU (Thanks Katman!)
  • P61 : 92-93 OBD-1 USDM GSR B17A DOHC vtec
  • PR3 : 92-93 OBD-1 JDM Integra RSi/XSi (B16A) ECU
  • PR4 : 92-93 OBD1 Integra RS/LS/GS (DOHC) scan (Thanks Katman!)

  • P72 : 94-95 OBD-1 Integra GSR (1.8L B18C? DOHC Vtec) identify scan funky AT ECU (Thanks Katman)
  • P74 : 92-95 OBD-1 Integra LS/GS (1.8L B18B) scan (Thanks Katman!)
  • P75 : 92-95 OBD-1 Integra LS/GS (1.8L B18B) scan (Thanks Katman!)

  • P08 : 92-95 OBD1 Civic/CRX VTi/VXi (sohc vtec) scan (Thanks Katman!) manual scan manual scan auto scan auto scan (Thanks eg6ajk)
  • P27 : 92-95 OBD1 Euro/Asian Civic (non-vtec 1.6l)
  • P29 : 92-95 OBD-1 JDM Domani (sohc zc)
  • P54 : 92-95 OBD-1 JDM Domani/1.8l Accord (1.8L B18B DOHC) scan scan (Thanks eg6ajk)
  • P70 : 92-95 OBD-1 Domani (sohc vtec zc - no oil pressure sensor reading)
  • P71 : 92-95 OBD-1 Integra (sohc vtec d16a)
  • P76: 94-95 OBD-1 JDM Integra (sohc zc)
  • P84 : 92-95 OBD-1 JDM Civic ETi (VTEC-E auto)
  • P91 : 92-95 OBD-1 JDM Civic Coupe (sohc vtec 1.6) identify scan (Thanks Katman)

OBD1 92-95 (ish) Prelude/Accord

Although not quite as similar as the Civic/Integra ECUs of this era, the Prelude + Accord ECUs are clearly from the same design group.
  • P0 A : 94-95 OBD-1 Accord EX
  • P0 C : 92-95 OBD-1 Accord 2.2L (??? F22B DOHC non-vtec)
  • P39 : 92-95 OBD-1 JDM Accord SOHC VTEC (F22A)

  • P11 : 92-95 OBD-1 Prelude 2.0i BB3 with F20A4 non-vtec
  • P13 : 93-95 OBD-1 Prelude Vtec (H22A DOHC Vtec) eprom check (Thanks Katman!)
  • P14 : 93-95 OBD-1 Prelude Si (non Vtec - H23A)
