.WelcomeGuest: TWiki is a flexible, powerful, secure, yet simple web-based collaboration platform. Use TWiki to run a project development space, a document management system, a knowledge base or any other groupware tool on either on an intranet or on the Internet. You can edit any TWiki page. The TWiki TM home is
at http://TWiki.org/
